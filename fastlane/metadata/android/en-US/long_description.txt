Battery Monitor is a simple, practical Android app for keeping an eye on your device’s battery.

It gives you clear battery information at a glance, shows useful notifications, supports configurable battery alarms, and lets you review battery history over time.

<b>Main features:</b>

- persistent notification with current battery level, status, and optional details
- on Android 16 and newer, the main notification can be replaced with a Live Update showing the current battery level or temperature,
- detailed battery information, including charge level, temperature, voltage, health, and power source
- configurable battery alarms: for full charge, low battery, or custom charge thresholds; temperature and battery health
- battery event history with filtering
- CSV history export for further analysis
- home screen widgets
- no personal data collection

<b>Battery status notification</b>

Battery Monitor can show a persistent notification with up-to-date battery information. You can choose what is displayed, such as battery percentage, charging status, or additional details.

<b>Battery alarms</b>

Set practical alerts to help you manage charging and discharging. Battery Monitor can notify you when the battery reaches a selected level, drops below a chosen threshold, or becomes fully charged.

<b>History and export</b>

The app records battery-related events so you can review how your battery status changed over time. Logs can be filtered and exported to CSV for further analysis.

<b>Widgets</b>

Home screen widgets let you keep important battery information visible without opening the app.

<b>Privacy</b>

Battery Monitor does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. It focuses only on local battery monitoring on your device.

<b>Project background</b>

Battery Monitor is a continued and rebranded version of Battery Indicator Pro / BatteryBot Pro. The project aims to preserve a classic, clear battery monitoring experience while keeping the app working well on modern Android versions.
